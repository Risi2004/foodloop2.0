# Driver role

Drivers pick up donations and deliver to receivers. Routes are under `/driver`.

## Main features

- **Dashboard**: assignments and status.
- **My pickups**: view accepted pickups.
- **Pickup**: pickup workflow for a donation.
- **Delivery** and **delivery confirmation**: complete deliveries.
- **Demo routes**: optional demo routing (when enabled).
- **Earnings**: driver earnings and payout requests.
- **Profile & edit profile**, **notifications**, **About**, **Privacy policy**, **Terms & Conditions**.

## Accepting pickups

Drivers accept pickups from available donations. During maintenance, accepting new pickups may be blocked while in-progress deliveries finish.

## Sudden maintenance

Sudden maintenance may wait for orders in `picked_up` or `in_transit` before the full maintenance experience applies to users.
