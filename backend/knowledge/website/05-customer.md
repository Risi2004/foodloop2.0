# Customer role

Customers shop on the FoodLoop marketplace. Routes are under `/customer`.

## Main features

- **Marketplace**: browse available food products.
- **Cart** and **payment**: checkout (online payment or cash on delivery where supported).
- **Order history** and **order tracking**: past and active orders.
- **Profile**, **notifications**, **About**, **Privacy policy**, **Terms & Conditions**.

## Subsidized access

Customers may register as normal or low-income shoppers. Low-income signup can require a GN division letter for subsidized access.

## Maintenance

During maintenance, new customer orders are paused. Use the FoodLoop AI chat bot or contact page for help.
