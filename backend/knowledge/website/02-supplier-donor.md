# Supplier (donor) role

Suppliers use routes under `/supplier` (legacy `/donor` redirects here). Roles include restaurant, supermarket, business, and individual donors.

## Main features

- **Dashboard**: overview and quick actions.
- **My donations**: list, create, and edit donation listings.
- **New donation**: add food with photos; AI may suggest category, quantity, storage (Hot/Cold/Dry), and suggested resale price in LKR.
- **Track order**: follow pickup and delivery status.
- **Digital receipt**: view donation receipts.
- **Earnings**: supplier earnings and payout requests (minimum payout amount applies).
- **Profile & edit profile**: update business or personal details and photos.
- **Notifications**, **About**, **Privacy policy**, **Terms & Conditions**.

## Donor obligations (from Terms)

Donors must provide food fit for human consumption per Sri Lankan food safety standards. Listings need accurate descriptions and photos.

## Maintenance

During active maintenance, new donations and claims are blocked. Existing in-progress deliveries may continue until completed.
