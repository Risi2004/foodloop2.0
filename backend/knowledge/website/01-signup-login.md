# Sign up and login

## Account types at signup

- **Receiver**: NGOs, food banks, and organizations that claim donated food.
- **Driver**: Volunteer or paid drivers who pick up and deliver food.
- **Restaurant / Wedding Hall**: Suppliers who donate or sell surplus food.
- **Supermarket / Groceries**: Suppliers who donate or sell groceries.
- **Individual / Startup**: Households or home businesses donating or selling food.
- **Customer**: Shoppers on the marketplace; low-income users may provide a GN division letter for subsidized access.

## Signup process

1. Choose account type on `/signup`.
2. Complete the registration form (details vary by role; may include documents uploaded to secure storage).
3. Verify email with OTP at `/signup/verify-otp`.
4. Some accounts require **administrator approval** before first login. If pending approval, login shows a message to wait for admin review.

## Login

- Use email and password at `/login`.
- After login, users are redirected to their role dashboard (supplier, receiver, driver, admin, or customer marketplace).
- Deactivated accounts cannot sign in; contact an administrator to reactivate.
- Forgot password: request OTP, verify, then set a new password.

## Signup during maintenance

Registration and login remain available during maintenance. Non-admin users see a maintenance screen after login while new orders are paused.
