# Receiver (NGO) role

Receivers are NGOs and organizations registered to claim surplus food. Routes are under `/receiver`.

## Main features

- **Dashboard**: overview of activity.
- **Find food**: browse and claim available donations.
- **My claims**: manage claimed items.
- **Track order**: follow delivery progress.
- **Digital receipt**: receipt forms for claimed food.
- **Profile & edit profile**: organization details.
- **Notifications**, **About**, **Privacy policy**, **Terms & Conditions**.

## Claiming food

Receivers claim donations through the platform. Matching considers proximity, urgency, expiry timing, and reliability patterns.

## Maintenance

During maintenance, new claims and orders are paused. The maintenance screen explains when service returns.
