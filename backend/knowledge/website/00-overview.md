# FoodLoop overview

FoodLoop is a food surplus redistribution platform for Sri Lanka. It connects suppliers (restaurants, supermarkets, businesses, and individuals), NGOs and receivers, volunteer or paid drivers, customers, and administrators.

## Mission

FoodLoop aims to reduce food waste at the source, redistribute surplus efficiently with tracking, and support NGOs with reliable data and resources.

## Public website

- Landing page: mission, statistics, and links to sign up or log in.
- Sign up: choose account type (Receiver, Driver, Restaurant/Wedding Hall, Supermarket/Groceries, Individual/Startup, Customer).
- Log in: email and password; forgot password flow with OTP.
- Contact page for support messages.
- Privacy policy and Terms & Conditions pages (before and after login, per role).

## AI features

- Donation listings can use AI-assisted food image analysis (category, quantity, storage, suggested price in LKR).
- FoodLoop AI Chat Bot on all pages answers questions about FoodLoop only, in English, Tamil, or Sinhala.

## Currency and region

Prices and payouts are in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR). The platform focuses on Sri Lankan communities and food safety expectations.
