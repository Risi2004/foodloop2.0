# Contact, privacy, and terms

## Contact

Use the **Contact** page (`/contact`) to send a message to FoodLoop support. Logged-in users may have name, email, and phone pre-filled.

## Privacy policy

Available on the landing page and within each role area. Covers how FoodLoop collects and uses data for the food redistribution service, including names of donors and NGO representatives where applicable.

## Terms and Conditions

Last updated January 2026. By creating an account or using FoodLoop you agree to the Terms and Privacy Policy.

Key points:
- FoodLoop is an AI-assisted marketplace matching surplus food with registered receivers in Sri Lanka.
- Matching considers proximity, urgency, expiry, and reliability.
- Donors must provide safe, accurately described food with photos.
- NGOs and receivers must use claimed food responsibly.
- Drivers must follow pickup and delivery procedures on the platform.

## FoodLoop AI Chat Bot

The chat bot answers only FoodLoop-related questions using official site knowledge and uploaded help documents. It supports English, Tamil, and Sinhala. It does not answer general knowledge, other websites, or unrelated topics.
